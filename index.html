Welcome to SnowCloud.ski


SnowCloud is a web and mobile platform in the snow sports industry offering a comprehensive reviews and ratings system for ski and snowboard instructors, as well as an instant online and mobile booking platform for lessons. Now, your guests can book and confirm their favorite instructors at the tap of a button and your snow sports school employees can put down the phone and get out on the snow!

How it Works


Powerful Tool to Enhance Your Guest Experience

Manage

SnowCloud's cloud-based platform makes scheduling, reservations, and sales more efficient and convenient for everyone at your resort. Partners can manage their entire snow sports school from any computer or mobile device and can complete sales and reservations from anywhere.

Instant Booking

Your resort guests love your instructors, but it's inconvenient to book with their favorite instructors over the phone or in-person. Partnering with SnowCloud enhances the guest experience by letting guests book and confirm a lesson with their preferred instructor for a specific date and time. Guests can interact with their instructor before, during, and after the lesson creating a deeper connection and increased likelihood for repeat visits.

Cloud Based

SnowCloud is a cloud-based platform. This means we never need to install costly software on your computers. We don't charge for individual software licenses and you can access the SnowCloud platform from as many computers, tablets, smartphones, etc, as you'd like! The devices constantly sync so that your schedules and sales are up to date on every device.

Interact

Enhance the engagement between your guests and your snow sports school by offering your guest a tool to directly book your instructors. Guests can chat with the instructor before the lesson to get information and advice before the start of the lesson, and interact after the lesson is completed to receive additional suggestions, tips, and to schedule future lessons!

On The Go

Using SnowCloud's mobile apps, your snow sports school can sell lessons from anywhere at the resort. Payment and administrative information is collected through the app, and the guest never has to go back to the school to sign up! Now, when you see a guest struggling and in need of a lesson, it's easy to approach them and offer to start a lesson at the tap of a button! If your guest realizes they need a lesson, they can also request a lesson through our apps from anywhere at the resort.
